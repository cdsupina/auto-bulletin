# My Daily Newsletter Interests

## Instructions

Copy this file to `interests.md` and customize it with your topics:

```bash
cp interests.example.md interests.md
nano interests.md
```

Be specific about what you want to track. Claude will search for recent news and articles about these topics daily.

## Example Topics

### Technology
- Latest developments in Rust programming language
- New releases and updates for Raspberry Pi
- Linux kernel security patches and updates
- WebAssembly ecosystem news

### Science
- SpaceX launches and missions
- NASA Mars exploration updates
- Quantum computing breakthroughs
- Climate science research

### Finance
- Cryptocurrency market trends

- Federal Reserve policy updates

### Local News
- [Your City] local events and news
- [Your State] government updates
- Weather alerts and forecasts

### Hobbies & Interests
- Maker community projects and tutorials
- 3D printing innovations
- Open source hardware developments
- DIY electronics projects

### Sports
- [Your Team] game results and news
- [Your League] standings and highlights

## Tips for Better Results

1. **Be specific**: "Rust 1.x release notes" is better than "programming news"
2. **Include context**: "Minnesota local news" is better than "local news"
3. **Name entities**: "SpaceX Starship" is better than "space news"
4. **Avoid too many topics**: 3-5 main areas work best for a daily digest
5. **Update regularly**: Add or remove topics as your interests change
